Welcome To RowBotix

Redefining Mechanisation, Reimagining Farming

About Us

Rowbotix is an Agtech startup, developing tractor driven and self-driving robotic farm equipment.

Our Story

Even before we called ourselves Rowbotix we were a group of people passionate about farming and farmers. We were searching for the window to serve the farmers and their cause in agriculture. We found our chance in 2016 and so the journey began! We decided to work and dedicate ourselves to designing such technology which is ahead of what is used right now

Our Vision

We want to develop solutions that are user-friendly, precise, and simple. We want to provide automated products to the farmers for their every possible need. While doing so, we want to establish ourselves as a brand that farmers trust.

Our Technology

Using simple embedded systems to ML & AI, we are developing robotic farm equipment that delivers precision, reliability, and operational flexibility.
